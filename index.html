Director's Message

If you ever need healthcare of any kind, we hope that you would make Suwa Shanthi Hospital your first choice based on our reputation for excellent clinical services, our facilities and our friendly Consultants and staff. Eye center

The Eye Unit of suwa shanthi Hospital consists of state of the art equipment for procedures such as cataract surgery. Further, our eye unit is manned by a well trained team of nurses who have years of experience in handling complex ophthalmic procedures. A panel of well known eye specialists visit suwa shanthi Hospital, Currently our Hospital eye unit has been identified as the preferred location for cataract surgery in Anuradhapura district.

Mother and baby care unit

Suwa Shanthi Hospital has been the preferred destination for baby deliveries in the Anuradhapura district with its exclusive combination of highly qualified staff and reputed specialist consultants. Currently suwa shanthi Hospital offers a highly price-competitive yet comprehensive baby delivery package. We offer a fixed price package for your delivery ( Caesarian) and free antenatal classes and a 10% discount on your baby's vaccination or medical investigations up to 5 years.

Cardiac unit

ECG ,Echocardiography & Exercise ECG Our cardiology department is designed to meet all requirements of Cardiologists and cardiac patients. Equipped with the facilities for ECG, 2D Echocardiography as well as Exercise ECG, the department is complete with a panel of well-known cardiologists and technicians, experts in the field, to aid your cardiac screening and management
